About

Here is a little story about us.

  • september 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The idea was started by two nasto traders, who had been currency traders in Bank Hapoalim (the largest bank in Israel). After 3 years in the banking industry they received an offer from a private businessman, to act as his private investors. This was the beginning of the road that lead them to learn all about investment opportunities, risk management and reports development.

  • DECEMBER 2016

    The Birth of Honey Investments

    After years of investing just for one man, the founders realized that they have the necessary knowledge and basic capital to open their own firm. With 7 years of experience and expertise in creating lucrative investment strategies, they decided to establish Honey Investments, primarily in order to enable others to financially benefit from their own expertise.

  • DECEMBER 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    Everything happened quite quickly. The founders created a business plan, developed a website, and have been adapting the Honey Investments platform in order to meet and satisfy clients’ individual needs. Most importantly, since its establishment, Honey investments has been working vigorously in order to built a nearly bulletproof trading strategy, which minimizes client-risk without significant adverse effects on potential profit. All these developments only took three months to become reality and Honey Investments is now prepared to embark on a new beginning towards a bright future

  • IN PROGRESS

    Phase Two Expansion

    We at Honey Investments see a bright future for our firm and our clients. We believe that everyone should have an opportunity to invest and benefit from their money, no matter how small the amount. We want to offer this opportunity to each and every person. We aim to become the first investment firm to collaborate with anyone who understands the financial potential of investing in the stock market, but has previously been unable to take part in this lucrative field due to lack of knowledge or insufficient funds.
